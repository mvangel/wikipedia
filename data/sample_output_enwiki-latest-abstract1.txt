title: Wikipedia: Albedo
abstract: Albedo (), or reflection coefficient, derived from Latin albedo &quot;whiteness&quot; (or reflected sunlight) in turn from albus &quot;white,&quot; is the diffuse reflectivity or reflecting power of a surface. It is the ratio of reflected radiation from the surface to incident radiation upon it.
